# Cleansing and Restoration – The Cleanser - சுத்திகரிப்பும் மீட்பும் – சுத்திகரிப்பவர்

Celebrates Jesus’ cleansing miracles: leper healed by touch (Matthew 8:1–4; Mark 1:40–45), ten lepers cleansed (Luke 17:11–19), paralytic forgiven then healed (Matthew 9:1–8; Mark 2:1–12), woman bent 18 years straightened (Luke 13:10–17). Shows Messiah as Cleanser of body and soul — forgiveness first, then restoration. Fulfills Isaiah 35:5–6 and Leviticus 14 (cleansing rituals).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, Cleanser and Restorer,
thank You for touching the unclean, forgiving sins, straightening the bent.
Cleanse us body and soul, restore our joy and wholeness.
In the eternal day, let us stand pure in Your presence.
Come quickly, our Healer and Redeemer.
