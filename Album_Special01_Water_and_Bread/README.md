# Jai Jesus Christ – Miracles of the Messiah - ஜெய் யேசு கிறிஸ்து – மேசியாவின் அற்புதங்கள்

## Water and Bread – The Provider - நீரும் அப்பமும் – வழங்குபவர்

This song celebrates Jesus’ miracles: water to wine at Cana (John 2:1–11, first sign revealing His glory), feeding 5,000 (John 6:1–14) and 4,000 (Matthew 15:32–39), walking on water (Matthew 14:22–33), fulfilling Isaiah 35:5–6 (blind see, deaf hear). All point to Him as Messiah and Provider.
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, Messiah and miracle-worker,
thank You for every sign that reveals Your glory.
Turn our ordinary into extraordinary, satisfy our hunger with Yourself, calm every storm.
In the eternal day, let us feast at Your table forever.
Come quickly, our Provider and King.
ஆமென். அல்லேலூயா!
