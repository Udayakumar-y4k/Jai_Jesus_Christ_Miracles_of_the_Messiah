# Storms and Sight – The Calmer and Opener - புயல்களும் பார்வையும் – அமைதிப்படுத்துபவர் மற்றும் திறப்பவர்

Song celebrates Messiah’s miracles: calming storm on Galilee (Mark 4:35–41 – authority over nature), healing blind man with mud & Siloam (John 9:1–7), Bartimaeus (Mark 10:46–52), widow’s son at Nain (Luke 7:11–17), fulfilling Isaiah 35:5–6. All reveal Jesus as Calmer of storms and Opener of eyes/ears.
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, Calmer of storms and Opener of blind eyes, thank You for silencing chaos and restoring sight. Calm our storms, open our eyes to Your glory. In the eternal day, let us see You face to face. Come quickly, our Messiah.
ஆமென். அல்லேலூயா!
