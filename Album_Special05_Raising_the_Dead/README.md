# Raising the Dead – The Life-Giver - மரித்தோரை எழுப்புதல் – ஜீவனளிப்பவர்

Celebrates Jesus raising the dead: widow’s son at Nain (Luke 7:11–17 – compassion moved Him); Jairus’ daughter (“Talitha cumi,” Mark 5:35–43; Luke 8:49–56); Lazarus after 4 days (John 11:1–44 – “Loose him”). Fulfills Isaiah 26:19 (“Your dead shall live… awake and sing”) and John 11:25 (“I am the resurrection and the life”).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, Resurrection and Life,
thank You for raising the dead and conquering the grave.
Give life to our dead places, call us forth from tombs of despair.
In the eternal day, let us rise in Your glory forever.
Come quickly, our Life-Giver.
